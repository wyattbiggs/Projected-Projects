Introduction (50-150 Words, 3-6 Sentences)
 - Why did you do the project? Does this relate to any other projects?
 - What is the project?
 - What are you trying to achieve?
 - What do you think will happen?

Methods (? Words, ? Sentences)
 - How did you do the project?

 - Explain the project chronologically. You can use a numbered list, section titles, or whatever other structure you'd like to differentiate steps or chapters of the project.
 - You don't have worry about making the story tidy, this can be done in the Results or Discussion sections if you'd like.

Results (75-150 Words, 3-8 Sentences)
 - How did your project turn out?

 - Describe the outcome of your project.
 - If there were any measurable outcomes of the project, share them here!

Discussion (50-150 Words, 2-5 Sentences)
 - Are you happy with the result?
 - What would you do differently?
 - Does this relate to any other projects?
